Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch between box types?

Yes! You can switch between box types at any time. If you're on a subscription, you can change your next delivery to a different box type through your account settings or by contacting our customer service team.

Do you ship internationally?

We currently ship to most countries in Europe, selected countries in Asia, and throughout New Zealand. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. During checkout, you'll see available shipping options for your address.

What if I'm going on vacation?

You can easily pause your subscription through your account dashboard. We recommend pausing at least one week before your scheduled delivery. You can also adjust your delivery schedule or have your box delivered to a neighbor.

Are the seeds suitable for my climate?

All seeds included in our boxes are selected specifically for your region. Our European, Asian, and New Zealand boxes contain different seed varieties suited to local growing conditions and seasons.

Can I give a box as a gift?

Absolutely! We offer gift subscriptions for all our boxes. You can choose the duration (1 month, 3 months, 6 months, or 12 months) and we'll include a personalized gift message with the first delivery.

What's your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your subscription at any time. For monthly subscriptions, cancellation takes effect after your current billing cycle. For prepaid subscriptions (3-month, annual), you'll receive any remaining boxes already paid for.
