About Oddbox

Oddbox was created to tackle one of gardening's biggest problems: food waste. Every year, millions of tonnes of perfectly edible fruits and vegetables are rejected by supermarkets simply because they don't meet cosmetic standards. They might be too big, too small, oddly shaped, or have minor blemishes - but they taste just as delicious!

Our weekly Oddbox brings these "wonky" vegetables directly to your door, along with the seeds to grow your own rescued varieties. You get fresh, seasonal produce while helping reduce food waste and supporting local farmers.

Oddbox Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly are "wonky" vegetables?

Wonky vegetables are perfectly edible fruits and vegetables that don't meet supermarket cosmetic standards. They might be oddly shaped, too large or small, have minor blemishes, or be surplus to requirements. They taste exactly the same as "perfect" vegetables but are often rejected by supermarkets for cosmetic reasons.

Can I choose what vegetables I receive?

Oddbox is based on seasonality and what's available from our farmers each week, so we don't offer custom selection. This approach allows us to rescue vegetables that would otherwise go to waste. However, you can indicate preferences or allergies in your account, and we'll do our best to accommodate them.

How do I store my Oddbox vegetables?

Each delivery includes storage instructions for the specific vegetables in your box. Generally, root vegetables (carrots, potatoes, beets) should be stored in a cool, dark place. Leafy greens should be refrigerated in airtight containers. Our weekly blog also includes storage tips and recipe ideas.

What if I'm not home for delivery?

You can leave delivery instructions in your account, such as leaving the box in a safe place, with a neighbor, or in a designated delivery box. We use insulated packaging to keep vegetables fresh for several hours after delivery.

Can I pause or skip a delivery?

Yes! You can easily pause your subscription or skip specific deliveries through your account dashboard. We require at least 3 days' notice before your scheduled delivery day to make changes.
