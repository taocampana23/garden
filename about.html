About Gardens

Growing communities across continents since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate gardeners in Amsterdam, Gardens began as a simple idea: to connect people with nature, no matter where they live or their level of experience.

What started as a local community garden initiative has grown into an international movement, serving gardeners across Europe, Asia, and New Zealand. We believe that everyone should have access to fresh, sustainable gardening opportunities.

Our unique approach combines traditional gardening wisdom with modern technology and sustainable practices, creating a community that learns, grows, and flourishes together.
